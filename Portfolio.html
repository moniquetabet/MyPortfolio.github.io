December 2022

Line Tracking
Robot

Line-tracking robot that detects a colored line and follows it. Arduinos were used to integrate RGB sensor information using C++, to control the brushed motors for smooth navigation. The TI MPS432, the PID motor controller was programmed using C. The Arduinos communicated with each other via I2C communication and communicated with the motor controller via UART.

December 2021

Toy Story
Themed Robotic competition

Me and my team designed and manufactured a robot that achieves 3 tasks: Raise Buzz's rocket, Rescue Buzz while avoiding traps, and deliver the ping pong balls in a rotating car. The system was controlled by an arduino in a C++ environment to control the motor for forward drive, activate a solenoid to drop the balls into the cavity, all while integrating the sensory data of switches and an ultrasonic sensor. A mouse trap was used to move Buzz out of danger. The team ranked #4 in the competion.
