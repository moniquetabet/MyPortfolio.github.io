January 9 - June 23, 2023

Amazon Robotics

Hardware Test Engineer

Ever wondered how your Amazon packages are handled in the Amazon Fullfillment centers? I had the opportunity to work on the sorting and consolidating robot!
I designed a test rig to autonomously test the end of arm tooling of the Robot, reducing testing time by 70%.
Using GD&T principles and Solidworks, I designed an electro-pneumatic test Rig to retro-fit the Robobtic part and all its sensors and actuators.
From ideation into creation, I manufactured my design, assembling all its components.
I designed the test plan for it, to avoid Dead On Arrivals, and to reduce operational bottlenecks.

I took on multiple porjects, one of which I developed a program in Labview from scratch, responsible of reducing Harware troubleshooting time by at least 50%, and reducing issue escalation.

May 9- August 5, 2022

RoviSys

Systems Engineer

Picture a world where factories and warehouses come alive with the magic of the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), and No. Human. Interaction. I helped achieve that!
I was responsible for creating ladder logic code in Studio 5000 by Rockwell Automation, which was deployed on Allen Bradley's PLCs. This code efficiently controlled field devices and monitored field input, ensuring seamless operations.
To enhance monitoring capabilities, I designed and upgraded HMIs using FactoryTalk View, enabling real-time tracking of sensor values throughout the facility. I also prioritized security by configuring device properties in FactoryTalk AssetCentre, maintaining normal device operation, and safeguarding assets.
One exceptional journey took me to an Amazon Warehouse, where I led a team of electricians and conducted a Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) to ensure optimal warehouse performance.
