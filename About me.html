Who am I

My professional introduction

I'm Monique Tabet, a senior Mechanical Engineer with a specialization in robotics at Georgia Tech.

My journey into the world of robotics and automation was fueled by a personal initiative to develop my computer science skills, driven by a fascination with the transformative power of automation. Beyond my core mechanical engineering curriculum, I proactively pursued coursework in C++, Java, and MATLAB, recognizing that the fusion of mechanical and computer science expertise is the key to pushing the boundaries of innovation in robotics. My quest for growth extends to all aspects of my life. My brain is wired to seek efficiency and optimization, not just in my academic pursuits but also in my personal endeavors. I leverage management tools and strategies to enhance productivity, streamline processes, and maximize results. This holistic approach reflects my commitment to continuous improvement and my readiness to make a lasting impact in the tech industry and beyond.

Our world is evolving at warp speed, and it's so easy to feel like you're caught in a whirlwind. But here's my little secret: I've found that life is all about balance.
